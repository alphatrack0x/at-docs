# Smart Portfolio

**Stop chasing your projects across channels. Track them, and let the market report to you.**

---

## The Idea

You hold tokens. You're watching others. Following each one across dozens of channels doesn't scale — so most people end up reacting to whatever happens to cross their feed.

Smart Portfolio flips that. Add the projects you care about, and your digests report what the market is saying about them — collected across channels, cleaned of spam, classified by AI. Your portfolio becomes your intelligence feed.

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your projects">
    Type a name or ticker — `arbitrum`, `SOL`, anything — and pick the match. Do it during onboarding or any time with `/projects`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="We track the chatter">
    Every mention of your projects across our channel coverage gets collected, filtered, and classified — the same intelligence pipeline that powers your digests.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your digest reports back">
    Project mentions arrive in your scheduled digests, processed by the same Alpha Agents — tagged by type, linked to source. What was said, by whom, with a link to verify.
  </Step>
</Steps>
