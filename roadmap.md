# Alpha Track Product Roadmap

**Building the Crypto Intelligence Platform**

*Last Updated: July 2026*
*Living document, updated quarterly*

---

## Vision Statement

We are building the intelligence layer crypto has been missing.

You track the projects you care about. We scan the entire internet, classify every mention by type (fact, opinion, emotion), analyze sentiment (bullish vs bearish), and show you what the market actually thinks. Real time. Any language. Any platform.

**What Makes Us Different:**
The entire platform is built and operated by AI agents. From collection to classification to sentiment scoring to delivery. We are not using AI as a feature. We ARE an AI-native platform. This allows us to scale intelligence that would be impossible with human teams.

**Guiding Principles:**
- Ship fast, iterate faster
- Community feedback drives priorities
- Depth over breadth
- Transparency always
- AI-native architecture from day one

## Where We Are Now (Q3 2026)

### Live: Telegram Intelligence Engine + Smart Portfolio v1

**What's Working:**

- **Smart Portfolio.** Track the projects and tokens you care about — digests report what the market says about them. 3 projects on Free, 10 on Pro, 100 on Premium.
- **Alpha Agents Message Tags.** Every digest message classified by type — fact, opinion, emotion — and tagged inline for paid tiers. The facts/opinions/emotions foundation is live in the product.
- **Telegram Integration.** Connect public channels you follow and get AI-processed digests.
- **6-Category Classification.** Influencer Insights, Activities, Global Market View, Exchange Promos, Summaries, IDO.
- **99% Spam Filtering.** AI removes noise automatically with near-perfect accuracy.
- **Universal Language Coverage.** Read channels in ANY language, get intelligence in English, Russian, Ukrainian, or Spanish.
- **4 Daily Digests.** Morning, midday, evening, night. Structured reports, not raw content.
- **Source Linking.** Every insight links to original message for verification.
- **Subscriptions.** Free / Pro / Premium tiers, monthly or yearly, paid in Telegram Stars or crypto. Every new account starts with a free month of Pro.

**Current Scale:**
Processing 10,000+ messages daily across universal language input with English/Russian/Ukrainian/Spanish output. Active beta with growing user base.

### In Development Now: Per-Project Intelligence Depth

Watchlists and project reporting are live. Now we go deeper on each tracked project:

**Per-Project Stats.** Mention momentum — how much the market talked about your project today vs yesterday — and sentiment trends over time.

**Narrative Tracking.** Follow themes and narratives ("AI agents", "RWA", "restaking"), not just individual tokens.

**Bullish/Bearish Scoring.** Full sentiment direction on top of the live type classification.

**Status:** Type classification live in digests. Per-project stats and narrative tracking in active development.

## Phase 1: Project Level Intelligence (Q3–Q4 2026)

*Focus: From categories to projects. From channels to the entire internet.*

### Project and Token Tracking

**The core feature that transforms the platform — v1 shipped.**

You add projects to your watchlist. We find everything the internet says about them. Not just your Telegram channels. Everything.

- **Custom watchlists.** ✅ Live. Track any project or token; your portfolio becomes your intelligence feed.
- **Automatic extraction.** ✅ Live. AI identifies tokens and project names across content.
- **Per-project stats.** In development. Mention momentum, sentiment trends, day-over-day deltas.
- **Dedicated project views.** In development. Everything about a project in one place: facts, opinions, emotions, sentiment trend, source breakdown.

### Twitter Integration

**Crypto alpha happens on Twitter publicly and Telegram privately. You need both.**

Same AI classification as Telegram applied to Twitter content. Track influencers across both platforms. Cross-platform synthesis in one unified view. Thread summarization. Narrative tracking.

### Sentiment Dashboard

**See what the market thinks at a glance.**

Bullish vs bearish ratio per project. Sentiment trend over time. Conviction breakdown (opinions vs emotions). Cross-platform sentiment comparison. Divergence alerts when signals conflict.

### Community Features

Shared watchlists and curated project collections. Channel leaderboards showing which sources provide the most value. Import/export your setup. Community-validated sources.

## Phase 2: Advanced Sentiment and Alerts (Q4 2026, Q1 2027)

*Focus: Deeper analysis, real-time signals, broader coverage*

### Full Internet Coverage

**Beyond Telegram and Twitter.**

Discord server monitoring. News site and blog scraping. Reddit and forum integration. Farcaster and Lens for Web3-native social. Research platform aggregation. Any source where people discuss crypto projects becomes an input.

### Alpha Alerts

**Real-time intelligence. Catch opportunities when they emerge.**

Detect unusual sentiment patterns: sudden bullish consensus building, sentiment divergences between platforms, influencer pile-ons, viral content in first minutes.

Get notifications when something significant shifts. Configurable thresholds so you control what triggers an alert.

### Conviction Analysis

**Go beyond bullish/bearish.**

Are the bullish voices making reasoned arguments or just posting emojis? Is bearish sentiment driven by analysis or fear? Track conviction depth, not just direction.

Influencer consensus tracking. See which analysts agree and disagree. Spot when the smart money narrative shifts.

### Cross-Language Intelligence

**Sentiment varies by region. See it all.**

Compare sentiment across English, Chinese, Russian, Korean, and other language communities. Regional divergences are some of the most valuable signals in crypto and completely invisible to anyone not monitoring multiple languages.

## Phase 3: Power Features and Platform (Q2, Q3 2027)

*Focus: Developer ecosystem, advanced tools, custom intelligence*

### Developer API

**Open our intelligence to builders.**

Public API for sentiment data, project intelligence, and classification output. Real-time webhooks. Historical data access. Custom integrations. Rate limits based on tier.

Use cases: trading bots, project dashboards, research tools, community platforms, portfolio trackers. Developers building on top of us equals distribution, moat, and revenue.

### Keyword and Narrative Tracking

**Track trends, not just projects.**

Custom keywords for emerging narratives ("AI agents", "RWA", "restaking"). Cross-platform tracking with sentiment per keyword. Dedicated digest sections. Monitor competitors. Follow macro themes ("regulation", "ETF").

### Predictive Signals

**From reactive to predictive.**

Sentiment trend forecasting based on historical patterns. Engagement anomaly prediction. Opportunity scoring. Risk assessment signals. Timing insights.

Built on the foundation of months of classified and scored sentiment data across thousands of projects.

### Analytics Dashboard

**Understand your intelligence diet.**

Time saved metrics. Sentiment accuracy tracking for sources. Channel and influencer ROI. Historical sentiment vs price correlation. Personal insight reports.

## Phase 4: Complete Intelligence Platform (2028+)

*Focus: The intelligence layer every crypto participant relies on*

### AI Research Assistant

Natural language interface:
- "What does the market think about Arbitrum right now?"
- "Show me projects where influencer sentiment diverges from community sentiment."
- "Which tokens had the biggest bullish shift this week?"
- "Compare sentiment on Solana between English and Chinese communities."

Multi-turn conversations. Source citations. Context-aware responses. Your personal crypto research analyst, available 24/7.

### Ecosystem Intelligence

**Map the relationships.**

Which projects are connected? Where does capital flow? Which communities overlap? Ecosystem-level intelligence that reveals patterns invisible at the project level.

### Full Predictive Analytics

**Sentiment as a leading indicator.**

Backtest sentiment signals against price action. Build custom models. Automate alerts based on predictive patterns. The quantitative layer built on qualitative intelligence.
